Everything you need

One app, a complete training system

From your first program to your next PR, SoSet handles the thinking so you can focus on the work.

AI-generated programs

Tell SoSet your goals, experience, schedule, and available equipment — it builds a complete program around them, not a generic template.

Daily readiness & adaptation

A quick sleep, soreness, and stress check-in lets your coach lighten, intensify, or reshape today's session before you start.

Focused workout logging

Log reps, weight, and RPE set-by-set with a clean, focused screen per exercise, rest timers, and instant progress at a glance.

PRs & progress history

Every personal record is detected automatically, alongside estimated 1RM trends, weekly volume, and a full training history.

Friends & community

Follow friends, see their wins on a shared feed, and climb a monthly leaderboard — with full control over what you share.

Chat with your coach

Ask training, recovery, or nutrition questions any time — your AI coach knows your program and history, not just generic advice.
