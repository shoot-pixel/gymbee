Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Welcome to SoSet Fitness (“SoSet,” “we,” “our,” or “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

By using SoSet Fitness, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted through our authentication provider)
  • Profile photo
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Height and weight (optional)
  • Fitness goals
  • Workout preferences

You may also choose to upload:

  • Progress photos
  • Before-and-after photos
  • Workout notes
  • Comments
  • Social posts

Workout Data

When using SoSet Fitness, we collect information including:

  • Exercises performed
  • Sets
  • Repetitions
  • Weight lifted
  • Workout duration
  • Rest periods
  • Personal records
  • Workout completion history
  • AI coaching interactions

Connected Health Services

If you choose to connect supported third-party services (such as WHOOP or Oura), we may receive information including:

  • Recovery metrics
  • Sleep metrics
  • Heart rate data
  • Readiness scores
  • Activity data

We only access data that you explicitly authorize.

Device Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information
  • IP address
  • Anonymous usage analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the app
  • Create your account
  • Save workout history
  • Display progress over time
  • Power AI coaching features
  • Recommend workouts
  • Improve app performance
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect fraud and abuse
  • Maintain app security
  • Send important account notifications

We do not sell your personal information.

Social Features

SoSet includes optional social features.

If you choose to share content, other users may be able to view:

  • Username
  • Profile picture
  • Workout activity
  • Progress photos
  • Before-and-after photos
  • Posts you choose to publish

You control what content you make public.

Photos and posts marked as private are not visible to other users.

AI Features

SoSet may use artificial intelligence to provide:

  • Workout recommendations
  • Exercise suggestions
  • Training insights
  • Performance summaries

AI-generated content is intended for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical or fitness advice.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers, including services for:

  • Authentication
  • Cloud storage
  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • Payment processing (if applicable)
  • Connected fitness platforms

These providers process information only as necessary to provide their services.

Data Retention

We retain your information while your account remains active or as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

You may request deletion of your account at any time.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

SoSet Fitness is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will update the “Last Updated” date when changes are made.

Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be sent to:

Email: privacy@soset.app
